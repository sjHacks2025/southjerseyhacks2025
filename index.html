About Hack South Jersey

Hack South Jersey (HSJ) is the first-ever high school hackathon hosted by the South Jersey Innovation Center (SJIC), and we’re thrilled to bring this opportunity to local students! On Saturday, September 27th, from 9 AM to 9 PM, high schoolers from across the region will gather for a day filled with creativity, collaboration, and innovation.

More than just a tech competition, HSJ is a place to work in teams, brainstorm ideas, and build real solutions together—whether you're a newbie or a coding whiz.

Plus, by joining our Discord, you'll stay updated on everything—team organization, workshop details, updates, and more!
